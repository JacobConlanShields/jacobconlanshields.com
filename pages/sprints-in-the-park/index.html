Sprints in the Park

Location: Cleveland Park, East Nashville

When: Saturdays @ 3pm

Everyone is welcome.

We run sprints on grass in the park.

"Bring someone you can beat, bring someone who can beat you, and their mothers!"

I am a personal trainer who likes to train sprints in the park.

I will also be doing calisthenics (bodyweight strength) and mobility.

This is a community event for anyone who wants to run sprints and race.

Don’t feel like racing? No worries. Mozy along at your own speed or train your own thing.
