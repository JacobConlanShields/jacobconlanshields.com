Sprints in the Park

Location: Cleveland Park, East Nashville

When: Saturdays @ 3pm

Everyone is welcome.

What we’re doing

  • Sprints on grass in the park
  • Calisthenics (bodyweight strength)
  • Mobility work

"Bring someone you can beat, bring someone who can beat you, and their mothers!"

About this

I’m a personal trainer and I like training sprints in the park.

This is a community event for anyone who wants to run sprints and race.

If you don’t feel like racing, no worries—mozy along at your own speed or train your own thing.
